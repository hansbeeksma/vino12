## Competitive Differentiation

| Area         | Vivino / Wine.com  | VINO12                  | Why Different                      |
| ------------ | ------------------ | ----------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Assortiment  | 10.000+ wijnen     | 12 wijnen               | Curated eliminates choice overload |
| Visual style | Generic e-commerce | Neo-brutalist           | Instant brand recognition          |
| Typography   | Sans-serif, clean  | Monospace, industrial   | Communicates authenticity          |
| Product info | Rating-heavy       | Story + tasting profile | Emotional connection over scores   |
| Navigation   | Complex filters    | Browse 12               | No filtering needed                |
